What If

Purpose:

KU What If is a website to assist both students and advisors alike, in measuring the progress of a student's major within Kutztown University. One of the biggest issues of college for a student is gauging progress of their major which often requires the oversight and assistance of an academic advisor. The KU What If application expedites the process for both the student and advisor by organizing their credits into an easy to read format. The website takes a student’s transcript, and uses it to compare the student’s academic progress to the academic requirements needed for any major, whether it is the student’s current major or a possible new major. It provides information on courses the student’s taken, credits they’ve fulfilled, and what other courses the student needs to take to complete the selected degree.

How to Use:

  • Upload unofficial transcript
  • Select your settings
  • Review the courses detected
  • Final version of transcript diplayed
  • Print results
